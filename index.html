    Here is my case study that I've done during my online courses





    E-commerce Website

    E-marketplace (like amazon, flipkart) where tribals of india can promote & sell tribal products such as tribal handicraft, paintings, minor forest produce ets online with provision of delivery, e-payment and promotional discount.

    Story behind the research

    This was the government project under the ministry of tribal affairs. When I was completing my UX design course, I also participated in smart india hackathon which was conducted in AUG 2020. I had total 6 members in my team from different area of expertise and my job was to design a smooth and user friendly UX and UI for our e-commerce website so that anyone who is totally new with the technology specially the tribal artists can also be able to use it effectively. We had to make the new e-commerce model but the difficulty arrived when we had to go one-on-one with the gaint competitors like amazon and flipkart with such expanded marketplace. The question was "what distinguishes us from others?"

    My roles and responsibilities

    I was the UX and interaction designer in my team. Other's were developers and presentors. It was my very first approach on whitch I began with the UX research where I had to study the whole ecommerce process from user intercations to the entire product cycle. Since it was my first approach to UX, I started with the e-commerce structure, the number of process takes a user to complete his task in the website and the time taken to complete the process. Every ecommerce sites have different interactions and time requirements so there are different users with different mental models. The goal was how can I can engage users to be in our website from start to finish?. How can I make sauch a mental model for the users which was not a big deal, but still it was all about less time, more smoothly.

    We worked on...

    #1. Delivery process

    #2. Networking among artists & dealers

    #3. Product packaging & maintaining

    #4. Ground report

    #5. Supporting unregistered tribals

    #6. Customization feature

    #7. Pricing & generating revenue

    #8. Augmented reality & personal profile

    Theme of our project

    The goal was to design user friendly interaction not only for end users but also for tribal artists so that they can keep track of their progress, communuicate with the customers directly using their personal profile where all the details are included. the obstackle we had to overcome was that we had to connect the user and the artists directly without the interference of middle man. which was the dealer(3rd party) of the tribal product so we did that using the personal profile of each artists. After that the next thing I had to work on was product and UI on the website which should be different than others. So it went like this...

    #1) Research
    #2) Creating business model
    #3) Defining user flow
    #4) Interaction design
    #5) Adding features
    #6) Final UI
    #7) User reviews

    We have used Augmented reality for Customer so that they can see the product they are about to see on their place and decide. All that just by scanning a QR code assigned to product visible with the product description Though your device need to be AR compatible for It to work.
    We made portfolio web section for the artisian who creates the product. This adds an emotional aspect to the Product. Promotes their work and them.
    Custom Product Option for user: It will allow the customer to give any special requested custom made product that they might want . For this there will be a separate reception and section. An executive will engage with them till the product is delivered for customer satisfaction.

    My approach to attract customers

    Using google adds

    The topmost benefit of Google AdWords is that it works faster than SEO. Both SEO and Google AdWords are search engine marketing strategies to generate more traffic and leads.

    Adding structured mark-up

    Structured data, also called schema markup, is a type of code that makes it easier for search engines to crawl, organize, and display your content.

    High CTR (click through rate)

    Including relevent and important imagery, keywords, call-to-action copy and ad positioning. The researchers in my team worked in finding best contents to generate high CTR.

    Adding relevant videos

    Adding lots of relevant videos like a product description video or product quality showcasing adds more imotional touch to the product.

    Product featuring

    Where customers can feature the product they bought from our website so that other custmers can directly find the purpose of their shopping experience.

    Personal gifting section

    Here customers can see their personal gifting suggestions based on their likes and purchase. Also recommending best gifts for better shopping experience.

    Targetting customers at the right place at the right time

    Yes, timing is important that which time customers are more likely to spend their time in online shopping whether it's festive season or different occassions.

    No dead ends

    Making sure that if a customer has added a product in their wishlist then it's gonna be always in the wishlist untill customer finally buy that product.

    Micro interactions

    Most ecommerce websites dosn't have this feature. It’s the little things that turn a good digital product into a great one. Makes it more pleasent and familiar.
